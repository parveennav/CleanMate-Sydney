How Much Does Commercial Cleaning Cost in Sydney?

Understanding commercial cleaning costs helps you budget effectively for professional cleaning services

Commercial cleaning in Sydney typically ranges from $25-$45 per hour or $0.15-$0.30 per square foot, based on facility size, frequency, and services required.

Factors Affecting Commercial Cleaning Costs

Several key factors influence commercial cleaning pricing in Sydney. Facility size is the primary consideration – larger spaces naturally cost more but benefit from volume discounts. Cleaning frequency significantly impacts pricing, with daily service offering better per-visit rates than weekly or monthly cleaning.

Scope of services affects cost considerably. Basic office cleaning (trash removal, vacuuming, dusting) costs less than comprehensive programs including carpet cleaning, window washing, and specialized sanitization. After-hours service may include a premium, typically 10-20% above standard rates, to accommodate businesses requiring evening or overnight cleaning.

Facility Size

Primary Pricing Factor

Larger facilities naturally cost more but benefit from volume discounts. Pricing scales based on total square footage of your space to provide fair and competitive rates.

Cleaning Frequency

Significant Impact

Daily service offers lower per-visit rates. Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly options are available, with customized schedules to match your operational needs.

Scope of Services

Key Cost Determinant

Basic office cleaning costs less than comprehensive programs including carpet cleaning, window washing, and specialized sanitization services.

Industry-Specific Requirements

Industry-specific requirements influence pricing as well. Medical facilities requiring healthcare-grade disinfection, industrial warehouses needing heavy-duty equipment, and food service establishments with strict hygiene standards typically have higher cleaning costs than standard offices.
